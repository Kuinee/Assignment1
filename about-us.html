About Us

Starry Services was set up with the aim to provide reliable one stop services to small and medium enterprises. Witnessing the frustration of securing right partners to cater to business needs; be it delivery, storage, logistic, insurance and other miscellaneous yet time consuming tasks; our team strives to provide SMEs the solution to their frustration.

We prioritise service quality and customer satisfaction, regardless if it is for personal or company. Let us be one of your reliable business partners which manage your non-core business activities such as delivery, storage and secretarial while you focus on your core business activities.
